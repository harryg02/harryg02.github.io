"ReentryGuide GR" is a mobile application developed in collaboration with Reentry Reimagined, designed specifically to support the reintegration of former prisoners into society within the Grand Rapids area.

The contract includes creating an inclusive and user-friendly mobile application for end users, alongside a website with documentation, encouraging people to fork the project and expand its impact.

The Challenge

Many of the app's users might have never interacted with modern technology or smartphones before. Additionally, users might have various disabilities, making it essential to prioritize simplicity and accessibility in the design process.
