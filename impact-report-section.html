Impact Report Design

Designed the 2024 Annual Impact Report, translating complex program metrics—including a 1% recidivism rate, 152,752 meals served, and 27 housing transitions—into a compelling visual narrative for donors and stakeholders. The report features custom data visualizations, infographics, and statistical presentations that communicate Exodus Place's mission and measurable impact.

Challenge: Transform spreadsheets of dry program data into a donor-facing document that tells a story of transformation and accountability.

Solution: Bold typographic hierarchy, clear color-coded sections, and strategic use of large numbers to create visual anchors. Each metric is contextualized with supporting imagery and explanatory text.

Design Approach

  • Visual Hierarchy: Large numbers (152,752 meals, 1% recidivism) serve as visual anchors, immediately communicating scale of impact
  • Color System: Brand-consistent green palette creates cohesion while maintaining readability
  • Data Visualization: Custom pie chart breaks down budget allocation with clear labels and percentages
  • Contextual Storytelling: Each statistic is paired with explanatory text and supporting photography to humanize the data
  • Accessibility: High contrast text, readable font sizes, and clear visual structure ensure the report is accessible to all stakeholders
